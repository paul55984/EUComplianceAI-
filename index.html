FAQ

Common questions.

Quick answers before you buy.

Does this apply to my business?

If you develop, deploy, or import AI systems in the EU — or your users are in the EU — the AI Act likely applies. Our classifier will tell you in minutes.

What counts as a "high-risk" AI system?

Key categories include AI used in hiring, credit scoring, education, biometric ID, critical infrastructure, and law enforcement. Our tool maps you automatically.

Is this a substitute for legal advice?

No. Our kit gives you frameworks and templates; you should have a lawyer review final documentation for your specific situation. Our Expert Review add-on can help.

What if the rules change before August 2026?

All kit purchasers get 6 months of free template updates. Scanner subscribers get automatic updates and alerts whenever implementation guidance changes.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee on the kit, no questions asked. Free trial for the Scanner, cancel anytime before you're charged.

Do you cover GDPR too?

Our documents are GDPR-aligned where the two frameworks overlap (e.g. data governance, transparency). A dedicated GDPR module is on the roadmap.
